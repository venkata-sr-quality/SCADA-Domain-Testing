# Quality Gaps – Lessons from Real Driver Testing

## Observed Patterns
- Incomplete test coverage due to ownership changes
- Test execution without review
- Late defect discovery during integration

## Root Causes
- Test status over test coverage
- Missing review checkpoints
- Unclear responsibility boundaries

## Preventive Practices
- Mandatory test case reviews
- Ownership tracking
- Release gates tied to coverage, not tasks

## Senior Insight
Most quality problems are process gaps,
not individual failures.
